= How Does Press Release Distribution Work? =

Press release distribution services are used to distribute press releases to news wires, journalists, reporters, social networks, news aggregators and other news outlets. A newswire is a service that distributes news, financial market updates, earnings reports and other company news or information. A news wire distributes news to various organizations, such as journalists, reporters, online newspapers, blogs, magazines, radio networks and television broadcasters. A news wire may also some times be referred to as a “news agency”. [How Does Press Release Distribution Work](https://prwirepro.com/how-does-press-release-distribution-work/ "How Does Press Release Distribution Work")
